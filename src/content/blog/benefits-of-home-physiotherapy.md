For many people, the idea of physiotherapy conjures up images of a clinical waiting room, a busy treatment table, and a rushed 30-minute appointment. But there's a better way.

## Why Your Home is the Best Place to Recover

Recovery doesn't happen in a clinic. It happens at home — on your stairs, in your kitchen, in your garden. When a physiotherapist assesses you in your own environment, they can see exactly what you're working with.

## Key Benefits

- No travel stress.
- Personalised to your environment
- More consistent follow-through
- Ideal for older adults and post-surgery patients
